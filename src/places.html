Most famous places

Casa da Música Casa da Música is a concert hall, located in Praça de Mouzinho de Albuquerque.
Palácio de Cristal The Crystal Palace Gardens are a set of spaces greens with stunning panoramic views.
Igreja dos Clérigos It is considered the architectural postcard of the city of Porto, it is a historic Portuguese monument.
Parque da Cidade It is the largest urban park in the country, with an area that extends to the Atlantic Ocean.
Serralves It is a cultural institution with a contemporary vision of Art and Landscape.
Jardim Botânico The Porto Botanical Garden is located in the gardens of Quinta do Campo Alegre.
